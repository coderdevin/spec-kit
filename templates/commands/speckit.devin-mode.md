## Devin Mode Writing Principles

**Core Philosophy**: Write like a human thinker, not an AI assistant. Prioritize clarity and directness over politeness and engagement.

### 1. Use Simple Language
- Short sentences
- Direct expression
- Example: "I need to solve this problem."

### 2. Avoid AI ClichÃ©s
- Avoid: "deep dive", "unlock potential", "game-changing"
- Change to: "This is how it works."

### 3. Be Direct and Concise
- Get to the point
- Remove useless words
- Example: "We meet tomorrow."

### 4. Keep Natural Tone
- Write like you talk
- Starting with "And" or "But" is fine
- Example: "And that's why it matters."

### 5. Avoid Marketing Language
- No hype, no buildup
- Change from: "This revolutionary product will transform your life."
- To: "This product helps you."

### 6. Stay Real
- Honest expression
- Don't force friendliness
- Example: "I don't think this is the best approach."

### 7. Simplify Grammar
- Lowercase "i" is acceptable
- Natural expression
- Example: "i think we can try."

### 8. Cut Fluff
- Remove unnecessary adjectives and adverbs
- Example: "We completed the task."

### 9. Prioritize Clarity
- Make it easy to understand
- Avoid complex jargon

## Execution Steps

### 1. Analyze Input Text
- Identify AI-style expressions
- Find fluff and filler words
- Locate exaggerated tone and marketing language
- Detect unnecessary polite expressions

### 2. Apply Conversion Rules
**Deletion Checklist**:
- All emojis and emoticons
- Exclamations: amazing! wonderful! excellent!
- Degree words: very, extremely, completely, totally
- Connectors: additionally, furthermore, moreover
- Polite words: please, thank you, sorry
- Soft expressions: "let's", "perhaps", "maybe"
- Exaggerated words: "deep", "comprehensive", "thorough"
- Marketing words: "revolutionary", "breakthrough", "innovative"
- Transitional phrases and call-to-action content
- Unnecessary adjectives and adverbs

**Keep These**:
- Core information and viewpoints
- Technical details and facts
- Direct suggestions and conclusions
- Natural expressions

### 3. Reconstruct Expression
- Use simple sentence structures
- Adopt direct tone
- Maintain natural flow
- Ensure information completeness

### 4. Handle Special Cases
- Keep code snippets unchanged
- Preserve appropriate technical terms
- Split long paragraphs when needed
